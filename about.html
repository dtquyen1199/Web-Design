Our Story

Founded in 2010, The Cone is the first non dairy ice cream shop in Glenferrie Shopping District.

To realize our philosphy "Ice Cream For Everyone", we make sure every allergens are listed on our menus and website.

Our Values

Our ice cream is made from 100% non-animal ingredients, for a more sustainable future for everyone.

Since 2010, we have commited to using only bio degradable spoons and cups for ice cream. Our ice cream is delivered in stainless steel tubs.

Our chocolate is made from certified sustainable cacao sources.

We want to bring our ice cream to everyone by making it dairy-free and being transparent about ingredients. Your little furry friends also have their own menu as well.
